House Rules

  • Check-in time is at 3:00pm.
  • Check-out time is at 10:00am.
  • The rate is $100.00 per night or $600.00 per week (prices subject to change for holidays).
  • Early check-in options are available:
  • From 10:00am at an additional cost of $40.00.
  • From 1:00pm at an additional cost of $20.00.
  • Late check-out options are available:
  • Until 12:00 noon at an additional cost of $20.00.
  • Until 2:00pm at an additional cost of $40.00.
  • Stays exceeding 3 days will require an additional cleaning fee of $50.00.
  • Pets are not allowed on the premises.
  • Parties are not permitted.
  • Smoking is strictly prohibited.
  • The maximum occupancy per room is 2 persons.
  • Cancellation Policy: A full refund will be provided if cancellation is made at least 72 hours in advance.
